Who We Are

About Us

The Future Square is a STEM-focused non-profit committed to bridging the gap between classroom learning and the real-world skills tomorrow's innovators need. We design accessible, hands-on programs for students, teachers and entire school communities.

◎

Our Mission

To make quality STEM education accessible to every learner — bridging the gap between classroom theory and the real-world skills our communities need to thrive.

★

Our Vision

A generation of confident, curious problem-solvers leading innovation in their schools, communities and industries — through learning that leads.
